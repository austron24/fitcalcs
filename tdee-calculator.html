Frequently Asked Questions

What is TDEE?

TDEE stands for Total Daily Energy Expenditure. It represents the total number of calories your body burns in a day, including all activities.

How is TDEE calculated?

TDEE is calculated by multiplying your Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) by an activity factor that reflects your daily activity level.

Why is TDEE important for weight management?

Knowing your TDEE helps you create the right calorie deficit for weight loss or surplus for muscle gain.
