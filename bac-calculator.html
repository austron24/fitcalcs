Frequently Asked Questions

How is BAC calculated?

BAC is calculated using the Widmark formula, which considers alcohol consumed, body weight, gender, and time elapsed.

What BAC is considered drunk?

Legal intoxication is 0.08% in most US states. Effects begin at 0.02% with reduced judgment starting around 0.05%.

How long until I am sober?

Your BAC decreases approximately 0.015% per hour. At 0.08%, it takes about 5-6 hours to reach 0.00%.
