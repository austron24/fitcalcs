Frequently Asked Questions

What is intermittent fasting?

Intermittent fasting is an eating pattern that cycles between periods of fasting and eating. Popular methods include 16:8, 18:6, and OMAD.

What can I have during a fast?

Water, black coffee, and plain tea are generally acceptable during fasting. These have minimal calories and won't break your fast.

When will I see results from fasting?

Most people notice weight loss and improved energy within 2-4 weeks of consistent intermittent fasting.
