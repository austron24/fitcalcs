Frequently Asked Questions

When do I ovulate?

Ovulation typically occurs around day 14 of a 28-day cycle, but can vary. It usually happens 12-16 days before your next period.

What is the fertile window?

The fertile window is the 5 days before ovulation plus ovulation day itself. Sperm can survive up to 5 days, making these your most fertile days.

How accurate is an ovulation calculator?

Ovulation calculators provide estimates based on average cycles. For more accuracy, combine with ovulation predictor kits or basal body temperature tracking.
