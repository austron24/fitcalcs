Frequently Asked Questions

How is ideal weight calculated?

Ideal weight is calculated using formulas like Devine, Robinson, Miller, and Hamwi, which consider height and gender to estimate a healthy weight range.

Is ideal weight the same for everyone of the same height?

No, ideal weight varies based on body frame, muscle mass, age, and gender. The calculated range is a general guideline.

Should I aim for my ideal weight?

Ideal weight is a reference point. Focus on overall health markers like body fat percentage, fitness level, and how you feel.
