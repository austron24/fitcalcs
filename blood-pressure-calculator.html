Frequently Asked Questions

What is normal blood pressure?

Normal blood pressure is less than 120/80 mmHg. Elevated is 120-129/<80. Stage 1 hypertension is 130-139/80-89.

When should I check my blood pressure?

Check at the same time daily, preferably in the morning. Sit quietly for 5 minutes before measuring.

What affects blood pressure readings?

Caffeine, stress, exercise, posture, and full bladder can temporarily raise blood pressure readings.
