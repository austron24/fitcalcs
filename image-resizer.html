Frequently Asked Questions

How do I resize an image without losing quality?

When reducing size, quality loss is minimal. When enlarging, limit to 200% to avoid pixelation. Use high-quality source images.

What is the best image size for web?

For web, keep images under 200KB. Common sizes are 1200px wide for content images and 150px for thumbnails.

Can I resize multiple images at once?

This tool handles one image at a time for best quality. For batch processing, consider desktop software.
