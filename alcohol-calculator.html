Frequently Asked Questions

What is a standard drink?

A standard drink contains about 14 grams of pure alcohol. This equals 12 oz beer (5%), 5 oz wine (12%), or 1.5 oz spirits (40%).

How long does it take to sober up?

Your body metabolizes about one standard drink per hour. No amount of coffee, food, or water speeds this up.

What is the legal BAC limit?

In the US, the legal driving limit is 0.08% BAC. However, impairment begins at much lower levels.
