Frequently Asked Questions

How much caffeine is safe per day?

Most adults can safely consume up to 400mg of caffeine daily (about 4 cups of coffee). Pregnant women should limit to 200mg.

How long does caffeine stay in your system?

Caffeine has a half-life of 5-6 hours. This means half the caffeine from your morning coffee is still in your system 6 hours later.

When should I stop drinking caffeine?

Stop caffeine consumption 6-8 hours before bedtime to minimize sleep disruption.
