Frequently Asked Questions

How do sleep cycles work?

Sleep occurs in 90-minute cycles consisting of light sleep, deep sleep, and REM sleep. Waking between cycles helps you feel more refreshed.

How much sleep do I need?

Most adults need 7-9 hours of sleep per night, which equals about 5-6 complete sleep cycles.

Why do I feel tired after 8 hours of sleep?

You may be waking in the middle of a sleep cycle. Try adjusting your bedtime by 15-30 minutes to align with cycle completion.
