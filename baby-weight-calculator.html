What does percentile mean?
If your baby is in the 50th percentile, they weigh more than 50% of babies their age. The 50th percentile is the average.
Healthy Range:
Most healthy babies fall between the 5th and 95th percentile. What matters most is consistent growth along their curve.

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are online health calculators?

Online calculators provide estimates based on general formulas. For personalized advice, consult a healthcare professional.

How do I calculate my daily calorie needs?

Your daily calorie needs depend on age, sex, weight, height, and activity level. BMR formulas provide a baseline, then multiply by activity factor.

What is a healthy BMI range?

A healthy BMI is typically 18.5-24.9. However, BMI doesn't account for muscle mass, so athletes may have higher BMI but low body fat.
