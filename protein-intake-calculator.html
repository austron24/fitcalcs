Frequently Asked Questions

How much protein do I need per day?

Most adults need 0.8g per kg of body weight. Active individuals and athletes may need 1.2-2.0g per kg for optimal performance and recovery.

When should I eat protein?

Spread protein intake throughout the day for optimal absorption. Aim for 20-40g per meal, with protein around workouts being especially beneficial.

Can I eat too much protein?

Most healthy adults can safely consume up to 2g per kg daily. Higher intakes are generally unnecessary and provide no additional benefit.
