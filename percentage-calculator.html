Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calculate percentage of a number?

Multiply the number by the percentage and divide by 100. For 20% of 150: (150 x 20) / 100 = 30.

How do I calculate percentage change?

Percentage change = ((New - Old) / Old) x 100. For example, from 50 to 75: ((75-50)/50) x 100 = 50% increase.

How do I find what percentage one number is of another?

Divide the part by the whole and multiply by 100. For example, 25 is what percent of 200? (25/200) x 100 = 12.5%.
